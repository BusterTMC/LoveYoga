-About Us-

A place to grow

We offer you a space and an atmosphere where you can grow as a person, wherever you are on your yoga journey.

Our History

Love Yoga was founded in 2001 by Buster. He was a student of the traditional Ashtanga Yoga and one of the first in Sweden to be authorized to teach.

Tradition

We follow the traditional Ashtanga yoga as it was developed by Sri K. Pattabhi Jois from the city of Mysore in India. We follow the tradition for ashtanga yoga to be as it is meant to be, a method for health and self-awareness.

Love

Maybe you easily get caught up in performance thoughts and need to learn to unwind. Maybe you need to find motivation and focus again. Perhaps you have injuries or disabilities that require rehabilitation. Maybe you long for meaning and community. With us, everyone is welcome. Here you get to develop at your own pace, together with others.
